Hridaya Shakti

Each one of us is on our own and unique journey in life. And in its course, we come across road blocks where we find ourselves wondering how to go ahead. Therapy comes in here to help us address these difficulties and work towards a meaningful solution. Entering into counseling or in to a psychotherapeutic relationship primarily involves gaining a deeper understanding and acceptance of oneself. This process can take place for an individual, couple, family or group. Hridhaya Shakthi, a center for Counseling and Psychotherapy is committed to a holistic and integrative approach to counseling and psychotherapy which affirms the active ongoing interrelationship between clinical experience and the spiritual reality of wholeness mediated through the energy of the heart to regulate mind, body and spirit. We are committed to the service of men, women and children who value the integration of their healthy spirituality with a healthy psychology set in the rich Indian religious and cultural tradition.
