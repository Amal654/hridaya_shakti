About us

Hridaya Shakthi means the energetic heart which animates our mind, body and self and not merely a pumping station of blood. From time immemorial people believed that heart had energy, a powerful energy and it conveyed deep wisdom. As human beings developed their brain, they began to lose sight of their heart process and emphasized the brain process. Hence, some people have either become too cerebral and others have become too emotional in responding to life’s exigencies that disrupt an authentic relationship with self, others and the world at large. Heart is an integrator or synthesizer of human experiences. Hridaya Shakthi envisages through psychotherapy and counseling, the heart should serve as the mediator of a new life, or life energy that heals the anxious brain, unhealthy body, fragmented self to relate with oneself, others and the world at large in healthy harmony. Through psychotherapy and counseling unhealthy and disruptive energy from the past is transformed into life energy that heals the whole person – body, mind and spirit – to celebrate life, to celebrate love, and yes, celebrate the mind that can come to know its heart.

Hridhaya Shakthi is a not-for-profit organization offering psychotherapy and counseling for adults and adolescents, families, individuals and couples. Our clinical staff who has been trained in Psychodynamic psychotherapy, Cognitive-Behavioral therapy and Family Systems as well as Structural and Emotionally-focused theories ensure the most effective and professional of services to our clients. The centre offers clinical services to individuals and couples, hailing from a variety of social, economic, and religious backgrounds and these services range from short-term, problem-focused counseling to long-term, in-depth psychotherapy.
